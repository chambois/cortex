What we do

Cortex was established in 1988 as an engineering, design, construct and consulting organisation to provide a wide range of services to the Bulk Materials Handling and Process Systems sectors. Since inception Cortex has undertaken major and complex multi-disciplinary design and construct projects involving mechanical, electrical, civil, structural, and control systems works throughout Australasia and South East Asia.

Cortex provides services in the design, manufacture, supply, installation and maintenance of systems in the areas of mining, building materials processing, production, materials handling, food processing, paper, chemical and petro-chemical industries

The combination of in-house skills and experience has enabled Cortex to provide total design, construction, maintenance and consulting services of complex installations.

Cortex is proficient in a wide range of project delivery, mechanical engineering design and consultant services as follows:

  • Prime Contractor
  • Partnering
  • Sub-contractor
  • Consulting
  • Efficiency Audits
