About

Cortex undertakes design and construction of major and multi-disciplinary Materials Handling and Process Systems projects in the Australasian and S.E.Asian regions. We deliver projects to the building materials producers, primary & secondary agricultural grain handlers, rail transport rolling stock positioning systems and industrial petro-chem, fertilizer, wood-chip, pulp & paper sectors. Cortex designs and constructs major port terminal infrastructure with road and rail unloading, silo storage, reclaim and ship-loading / unloading capabilities.
