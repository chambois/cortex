Port Adelaide Ship loader

CORTEX Resources Pty Ltd designed and constructed a mobile 1000tph mineral sands handling ship-loading system for Flinders Ports venture and delivered it to operate at Inner Harbor, Port Adelaide, South Australia. The system includes:

  • Cortex mobile tip truck dump feeder
  • Cortex mobile rubber tyred tricycle wheel mounted self-propelled ship-loader
  • DSI designed Snake Sandwich belt conveyor high angle elevator

The design and construction was carried out under Cortex's responsibility to Flinders Ports. It included the Snake Sandwich high angle belt elevator system technology developed by Dos Santos International of the USA. Cortex constructed the Snake Sandwich system under DSI's supervision and assembled it within the Cortex mobile ship-loader assembly.

The system is rated at 1000tph and is totally relocatable anywhere on the wharf and can be configured as a left or right hand feeder to loader arrangement to suit the ship berth location, hold location and the tip truck traffic flow.
