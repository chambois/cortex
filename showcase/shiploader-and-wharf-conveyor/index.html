Ship loader and wharf conveyor

CORTEX RESOURCES PTY LTD designed and constructed the 2500 tph (wheat) Wharf conveyor complete with tripper car to deliver grain to the rail mounted Shiploader at Outer Harbor, Port Adelaide, South Australia. The system includes:

  • 250M long Wharf Conveyor and Gallery (Bund mounted landside)
  • Tripper Car to deliver grain to the Shiploader
  • Shiploader - rail mounted 2500 tph capacity
  • Cascade chute
